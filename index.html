What is the rule of Andar Bahar?

Here are the simple rules of Andar Bahar:

Objective: Predict whether the next card will match the first card on the "Andar" (inside) or "Bahar" (outside) side.

Starting the Game: A card is drawn from the deck and placed in the center.

Betting: Players place their bets on either "Andar" or "Bahar.

Dealing Cards: Cards are dealt one by one to the Andar and Bahar sides alternately.

Winning: The first side (Andar or Bahar) that gets a card matching the center card wins.

Payout: Winning bets on the correct side are paid out based on the odds.
